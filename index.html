SERVICES

Michael G. Parent Law Corporation provides a number of family law services, including marriage annulments, custody, divorce, and litigation. Many clients appreciate our style; first, we always look for a sound, reasonable solution, but will put our litigation experience to use when necessary. Michael G. Parent Law Corporation also offers fee payment plans, making our services affordable for a majority of clients. In addition to Surrey and Langley, we can attend courthouses throughout the Lower Mainland, from Chilliwack to Vancouver.

Family Law Consultations & Advice

We can provide family law consultations and advice in all areas of family law, including: Guardianship under the Family Law Act (BC) Custody under the Divorce Act (Canada) Parental Responsibilities & Parenting Time Co-Parenting Child Protection Concerns Grandparent Rights Financial Disclosures Child Support Spousal Support Division of Property & Debts Divorce & Annulment

Court Advocacy in the BC Lower Mainland

Michael G. Parent Law Corporation can advocate on your behalf in situations such as: Trials in Provincial Family Court & Supreme Court of BC Applications to Obtain Court Orders Variation of Existing Orders or Agreements Enforcement of Agreements or Orders

Negotiation & Preparation of Written Agreements

We can help ensure your negotiations and written agreements are legally sound. Some of our negotiation and agreement preparation services include: Cohabitation & Marriage Agreements Separation Agreements Variation Agreements

Alternate Dispute Resolution

As a law firm that values the collaborative law process, we initially seek to resolve disputes through mediation and collaborative settlements, using litigation only as a last resort.
