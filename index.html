What Is Alternative Family Dispute Resolution?

The Family Law Act of British Columbia encourages the use of out-of-court dispute resolution processes — Family Dispute Resolution — where appropriate. Lawyers are required to make their clients aware of the most appropriate and effective services for resolving their disputes, based on the clients’ circumstances. These services include mediation, collaboration, and structured negotiation.


Many clients find the alternative processes to be a better alternative to court-based resolutions as it is often less stressful and can better serve the interests of all parties involved.
